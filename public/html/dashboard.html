BePrepared!

You activated the BePrepared! disaster mode.
Enter your remaining food under Food List.
Enter the people you have to distribute it to under Users.
You will be able to see a disaster food rationing plan under Disaster situation.
You will also see a forecast of how many days you will survive.

TIPPS

Try to contact a rescue team and inform them about the time you can survive.
Move as little as possible to save energy.
Try to keep warm.

If you can't cook your food, try to conserve it with salt, vinegar or sugar!
